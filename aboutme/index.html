About Me

Whitney Hansen

Hi everyone, I'm Whitney! I am from Chester, Idaho, and I am the third of eleven children, so I was raised in a large family [lots of chaos!]. I am not married myself, but I do know I don't want that many kids when I get around to marriage.

I am studying Geospatial Computing, so I work with GIS [Geographical Information Systems]. I'm currently doing an internship at BYU-Idaho working with Indoors GIS for campus, which is an incredible opportunity. Along with mapmaking, I am also a huge nerd for Geology, so I like to go hiking and rock hunting when it is warm outside.

I have been a member of the church for my entire life, which I am very grateful for. I've been blessed in many ways, and I am always grateful that my upbringing was so beautiful.

Although I don't believe web design is a necessary skill for my career, I do believe it to be important to my future regardless. There is a lot to be learned from web design, and I do think it is incredible how bits of code can turn into a beautiful website. I am excited to learn more!

Thank you for reading, and I am looking forward to getting to know you all!
